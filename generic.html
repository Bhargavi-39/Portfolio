I’m a Business/Data Analyst passionate about bridging the gap between strategy and execution. My work lives at the intersection of data, process, and storytelling—helping teams move from messy inputs to meaningful results.

I’m a Business/Data Analyst who thrives on turning complexity into clarity. With a Master's degree in Information Systems in Pace University from New York City, New York. My career has taken me across Financial services, Payments and Insurance domains, where I’ve built a reputation for translating ambiguous business needs into testable requirements, traceable data flows, and dashboards that leaders actually use. Whether it’s SQL data cleaning, building BI models in Power BI and Tableau, or designing wireframes and BPMN diagrams, I bring both rigor and creativity to every project. My strength lies in bridging strategy with execution—ensuring that business intent, system design, and data outcomes align without gaps.

At the same time, I approach analysis with curiosity and enthusiasm. I like to think of myself as half detective, half storyteller: I chase down data clues, validate them with stakeholders, and present them as narratives that drive decisions. Serious about my career but never dull, I aim to make policies executable, dashboards believable, and processes survivable. For me, success isn’t just about crunching numbers—it’s about creating solutions that are auditable, insightful, and lasting, while still keeping stakeholders engaged and smiling through the process.
